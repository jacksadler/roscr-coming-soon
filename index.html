Rotating Savings and Credit Associations (ROSCA's) are an age old system of sharing capital, still used regularly across the globe to provide a social atmosphere for people to lend and borrow within a trusted community.


At ROSCr, we provide the platform for you and your trusted group. We provide a simple savings group and make it available without the need for high interest loans and credit.
